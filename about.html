Who We Are

Our Story

Recruitment as a relationship — not a transaction.

2017 Founded in Faisalabad by Eng. Muhammad Rashid

Father & Sons Manpower Supply began with a simple conviction: that a placement is the start of a relationship, never the end of a deal. From our Faisalabad base we built an organized, Government-approved recruitment service around three things clients could count on — reliability, commitment and creativity.

Today we source, screen and deploy skilled and semi-skilled manpower across construction, engineering, energy, healthcare and hospitality. Every profile is trade-tested and fully documented before it reaches an employer, and every deployment is handled to Government and client standard.

“We measure success in repeat business, not single placements. That is the family philosophy — and it hasn't changed since day one.”

What sets us apart is what happens after the crew is on site. We follow up on every placement — for the employer's peace of mind and the candidate's wellbeing — because sustained growth, for our clients and for us, is built one kept promise at a time.
